About FidgetDesk

What is FidgetDesk?

FidgetDesk is an interactive web application that simulates a digital desktop filled with objects users can click, drag, and interact with. It is designed to provide a simple and engaging experience directly in the browser.

Why was it created?

This project was created to explore front-end web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It focuses on building interactive elements and creating a user-friendly experience while also serving as a fun way to take short mental breaks during work or study.

Who is it for?

FidgetDesk is intended for students, professionals, and anyone looking for a quick, interactive distraction. It works on both desktop and mobile devices, making it accessible anywhere.
